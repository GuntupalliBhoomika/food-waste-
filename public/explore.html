About Our Partner NGOs

Food Waste Reduction Alliance

We are proud to be partnered with Food Waste Reduction Alliance, who work tirelessly to distribute surplus food to local communities and reduce hunger. Their programs focus on food recovery, preparation, and redistribution to food banks and shelters.

ReFED

ReFED specializes in food recovery from local grocery stores, redirecting unsold but still good food to food banks and other charitable organizations. Their programs also focus on creating awareness about food waste.

Feeding America

Feeding America focuses on providing nutritious meals for homeless shelters by recovering surplus food from restaurants and grocery stores. They aim to reduce food waste and provide meals for those who are food insecure.
